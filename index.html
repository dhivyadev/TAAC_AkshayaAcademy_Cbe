About Us

Welcome to TAAC

Welcome to TAAC | Akshaya Academy Campus, where excellence meets innovation in education. We take pride in being an One of the top 5 CBSE Schools in Tamilnadu, that transcends traditional boundaries to provide a holistic and futuristic learning experience for our students.

Nestled in the Heart of Innovation and Education, TAAC stands as a beacon of excellence, redefining the conventional approach to schooling. Our commitment to providing an unparalleled education experience is reflected in our enhanced CBSE curriculum.

With a focus on Early NEET and JEE training, we aim to empower our students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in competitive examinations.
